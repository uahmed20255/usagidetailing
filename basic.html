BASIC PACKAGE

Essential Care for Your Vehicle

Our Basic Detailing Package is perfect for those who want to keep their vehicle clean and well-maintained without the need for an extensive service. This package covers both interior and exterior detailing, providing a comprehensive cleaning to refresh your car’s appearance.

What’s Included:

  • Exterior Wash & Dry: A thorough hand wash to remove dirt, grime, and road contaminants from the exterior of your car, followed by a hand dry to prevent water spots.
  • Tire & Wheel Cleaning: Cleaning and shining your tires and wheels, removing brake dust, dirt, and grime.
  • Steam Cleaning: We use steam cleaning for interior surfaces to remove deep-seated dirt, stains, and bacteria from upholstery, carpets, and mats, leaving a sanitized finish.
  • Interior Vacuuming: We vacuum the seats, carpets, floor mats, and other areas to remove dirt, dust, and debris.
  • Dashboard & Console Cleaning: We wipe down and clean the dashboard, console, and other hard surfaces, restoring their shine and removing fingerprints.
  • Window & Mirror Cleaning: We ensure your windows and mirrors are spotless, improving visibility and providing a crystal-clear view.

Starting Prices:

  • Sedans: Starting at $75
  • SUVs/Trucks: Starting at $100

Why Choose the Basic Detailing Package?

The Basic Detailing Package is a great way to maintain your car’s cleanliness on a regular basis. It’s designed to cover the essential cleaning services both inside and out, keeping your vehicle looking fresh and well cared for without breaking the bank.

If you're looking for a no-fuss, thorough clean, this is the ideal package for you. For a deeper level of care, be sure to check out our Full Detailing Package or Premium Detailing Package.

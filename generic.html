Basic Detailing Package

Our Basic Detailing Package is perfect for those who want to keep their vehicle clean and well-maintained without the need for an extensive service. This package covers both interior and exterior detailing, providing a comprehensive cleaning to refresh your car’s appearance.

What’s Included:

  • Exterior Wash & Dry: Hand wash to remove dirt and grime, followed by a hand dry.
  • Tire & Wheel Cleaning: Cleaning and shining your tires and wheels.
  • Steam Cleaning: Deep clean for upholstery, carpets, and mats.
  • Interior Vacuuming: Full vacuum of seats, carpets, and mats.
  • Dashboard & Console Cleaning: Wipe down and restore shine.
  • Window & Mirror Cleaning: Ensure spotless windows and mirrors.

Starting Prices:

  • Sedans: Starting at $75
  • SUVs/Trucks: Starting at $100

The Basic Detailing Package is a great way to maintain your car’s cleanliness on a regular basis. For a deeper level of care, check out our Full or Premium packages.

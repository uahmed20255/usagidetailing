Full Detailing Package

Comprehensive Care for Your Vehicle

Our Full Detailing Package is designed for customers who want a deeper, more thorough cleaning of both the interior and exterior of their vehicle. This package offers everything in our Basic Detailing Package, along with additional services that restore and enhance your car’s appearance inside and out.

What’s Included:

  • Exterior Wash & Dry: A complete hand wash to remove dirt, grime, and road contaminants, followed by a careful dry to prevent water spots.
  • Tire & Wheel Cleaning: Thorough cleaning of your tires and wheels, removing brake dust, grime, and leaving a polished shine.
  • Clay Bar Treatment: A special treatment to remove embedded contaminants from the paint, leaving it smooth and glossy.
  • Steam Cleaning: Deep steam cleaning of your car’s interior to remove dirt, stains, and bacteria from upholstery, carpets, and mats, leaving the interior sanitized.
  • Interior Vacuuming: Full vacuuming of all carpets, seats, and floor mats to remove debris, dust, and dirt.
  • Dashboard & Console Cleaning: We clean and polish your dashboard, console, and other surfaces, removing fingerprints and restoring shine.
  • Window & Mirror Cleaning: Cleaning of all windows and mirrors for a streak-free, clear view.
  • Interior Fabric/Leather Cleaning: We clean and treat your car’s fabric or leather seats, making them look fresh and comfortable.
  • Pet Hair Removal: Special attention to removing pet hair from your car’s seats, carpets, and upholstery, leaving your interior spotless.
  • Air Freshener: We leave your car smelling fresh with an air freshener of your choice.

Starting Prices:

  • Sedans: Starting at $150
  • SUVs/Trucks: Starting at $200

Why Choose the Full Detailing Package?

The Full Detailing Package is the ideal choice for those who want a complete, deep clean. It covers both interior and exterior, restoring your car’s shine and cleanliness with added services. Whether you need a thorough cleaning for your daily vehicle or preparation for a special event, this package ensures your car looks and feels its best.

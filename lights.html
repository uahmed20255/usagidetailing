Headlight Restoration

Clear Your Lights, Enhance Your Safety

Is your vehicle’s visibility compromised by foggy, yellowed, or dirty headlights? Our headlight restoration service restores clarity to even the most faded headlights, improving both the appearance of your car and your driving safety.

Our Headlight Restoration Process:

  • Sanding: We begin by carefully sanding the surface of your headlights to remove oxidation and dirt buildup.
  • Polishing: After sanding, we polish the headlights to restore their clarity and shine, removing any remaining haze.
  • Coating:
    Spray-On Headlight Coating: Premium protection against oxidation and yellowing for long-lasting clarity.
    Ceramic Coating: A budget-friendly option that ensures your headlights remain clear and fade-resistant.

Benefits of Headlight Restoration:

  • Improves visibility and driving safety.
  • Enhances your car’s appearance.
  • Reduces the need for expensive headlight replacements.

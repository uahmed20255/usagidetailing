Premium Detailing Package:

The Ultimate Care for Your Vehicle

Our Premium Detailing Package is the most comprehensive service we offer, designed to provide a complete restoration for your vehicle. This package includes all the services from the Full Detailing Package, plus additional high-end treatments that ensure your car looks and feels brand new.

What’s Included:

  • Exterior Wash & Dry: A complete hand wash followed by a hand dry to remove dirt, grime, and contaminants, leaving your car spotless. Click Learn More About Ceramic Coating
  • Ceramic Coating: Advanced protection for your car’s exterior with a ceramic coating that offers long-lasting shine, durability, and resistance to environmental contaminants. Click to Learn About Ceramic Coating
  • Interior Steam Cleaning: Deep steam cleaning to remove dirt, bacteria, and stains from upholstery, carpets, and mats, leaving your car’s interior spotless and sanitized.
  • Interior Vacuuming: Full vacuuming of all seats, carpets, floor mats, and other areas.
  • Dashboard & Console Cleaning: Detailed cleaning and restoration of your dashboard, console, and all hard surfaces.
  • Leather & Fabric Treatment: We clean, condition, and protect both leather and fabric seats, ensuring they stay fresh and comfortable.
  • Pet Hair Removal: Special attention to removing stubborn pet hair from all seats, carpets, and upholstery.
  • Plastic Shine Treatment: We restore the shine to all plastic surfaces inside your vehicle, including mats, trim, and faded plastic or rubber parts, giving them a like-new appearance.
  • Window & Mirror Cleaning: Comprehensive cleaning of your car’s windows and mirrors, leaving a streak-free finish.
  • Air Freshener: We leave your car smelling fresh with a scent of your choice.

    • SUVs/Trucks: Starting at $400

    Why Choose the Premium Detailing Package?

    The Premium Detailing Package is the ultimate choice for those who want to give their car the best possible care.
